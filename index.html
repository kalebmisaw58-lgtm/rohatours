Historical Masterpieces

Discover the pillars of the North as separate, immersive private expeditions.

3 Days • Spiritual Marvel

Lalibela Churches

The "New Jerusalem." Explore 11 monolithic churches carved entirely from volcanic rock in the 12th century.

2 Days • Royal History

Gondar Castles

The "Camelot of Africa." Visit the Royal Enclosure (Fasil Ghebbi) and the iconic Debre Berhan Selassie church.

2 Days • Ancient Empire

Axum Empire

The birthplace of Ethiopian civilization. Stand before giant obelisks and the purported home of the Ark of the Covenant.

10 Days • Culture

Omo Valley Tribes

A deep dive into the lives of the Southern tribes including the Hamer and Mursi people.

4 Days • Geology

Danakil Adventure

The hottest place on earth. See the Erta Ale lava lake and the colorful Dallol sulfur fields.

3 Days • Heritage

Harar Jugol

Experience the walled city of Harar, the hyena men, and a labyrinth of vibrant alleyways.
